About Lindsay

Lindsay is a licensed speech‑language pathologist with focused training and experience in adult cognitive communication and language disorders. She has a unique background across inpatient rehabilitation and outpatient therapy, and as a native Floridian from Martin County, she is passionate about serving adults in her local community.

Clinical Approach

Lindsay’s work is grounded in evidence‑based practice and centered on functional goals. Sessions are structured and tailored to what matters most: everyday conversations, managing home and finances, returning to work, and participating fully in life.

  • Functional, real‑world communication goals
  • Evidence‑based assessment and treatment
  • Close collaboration with families and care teams
  • Flexible in‑person and telehealth visit options

Education & Credentials

  • B.S. in Communication Sciences and Disorders – University of Central Florida
  • M.S. in Speech‑Language Pathology – Eastern New Mexico University
  • Certificate of Clinical Competence (CCC‑SLP), ASHA
  • Licensed Speech‑Language Pathologist – Florida
  • Extensive experience with adult neurological populations
  • Additional training in cognitive rehabilitation & aphasia care
  • Ongoing contributor to a local stroke support group for over eight years in collaboration with Cleveland Clinic and Encompass Health

Specialized training includes SPEAK OUT!®, Ampcare, and certification as a Lingraphica specialist for speech‑generating communication devices.
